HOW TO PARTICIPATE:

- Obtain a Jersey either with the quest provided by Ursor (Alsius), Mauro (Syrtis) or Saniur (Ignis) or buying it with Ximerin.
- With the Jersey equipped, you will sum points that will be added to this Ranking.

HOW TO OBTAIN POINTS:

- Defeating an enemy or helping to do so, as long as it is not from the same national team and is not lower than 5 levels from your level (1 point).
- Participating in the capture of a fort (40 points). Obtainable only once a day.
- Completing a clan objective (25 points). Obtainable only once a day.
- Defeating one of the bosses of the world cup (40 points). Obtainable only once a day.
- Participating in a successful stealing of a realm gem (200 points). Obtainable only once a week.

PRIZES:

- Every player that participates of the tournament and gets to 200 points will automatically obtain a 30 day White Lion Ancient mount.
- Every player that participates of the tournament and gets to 1000 points will automatically obtain a Platinum lucky box.
- Each national team in a group will have 9 winners (player team): the best Archer, Mage and Warrior of each Realm.
- The national team in the first place, will get an Amulet of the Champion and a Ring of the Champion for each one in the player team. The best of each class will also get 10000 Ximerin
- The other national teams will get a Ring of the Champion for each one in the player team.

DURATION:

- From June 20th, 10 AM GMT to July 15th, 10 AM GMT.

There will be more secret prizes and surprises during the whole event!

WARNING: Those players that break any of the terms of use or rules of conduct will obtain a red card and will be disqualified from the tournament. Only fair play!
