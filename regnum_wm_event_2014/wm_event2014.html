HOW TO PARTICIPATE:

- Obtain a Jersey either dropping it from the champions in the War Zone (El Diego, O Rei, The Kaiser or Zizou) or buying it with Ximerin.
- With the Jersey equipped, you will sum points that will be added to this Ranking.

PRIZES:

- Each national team in a group will have 9 winners (player team): the best Archer, Mage and Warrior of each Realm.
- The national team in the first place, will get an Amulet of the Champion and a Ring of the Champion for each one in the player team. The best of each class will also get 5000 Ximerin
- The other national teams will get a Ring of the Champion for each one in the player team.

SPECIAL RAFFLE:Those that have the jerseys of the semi-finalists (Argentina, Brazil, Germany or Netherlands) will enter in a raffle for Ximerin. 10 prizes will be raffled on Monday 14th of July at 10 AM GMT.
- The Ximerin amount for each of the 10 winners will be what the winner character has spent since 21st of June and 13th of July. If no Ximerin was spent or the amount of Realm Points obtained during those dates is higher, the prize will be that amount in Ximerin.

DURATION:

- From June 21st, 9 AM GMT to July 13th, 9 AM GMT.

There will be more secret prizes and surprises during the whole event!
WARNING: Those players that have enemy casualties near an Altar of Resurrection will be completely disqualified.
